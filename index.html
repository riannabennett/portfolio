About Me

I am seeking opportunities at a design centric consumer product company with a strong learning and experimentation culture. In addition to continuing to hone my design aesthetic, my goals this year include learning CSS frameworks, collaborating with full stack tech teams, and running (another!) marathon. 


My idea of great product design combines a user centered approach with a strong and appealing visual language. My formal education in architecture has ingrained product thinking into each design problem that I encounter. As a very hands-on designer, I enjoy being involved in the entire design process, starting from ideation and conceptualizing to designing, prototyping, testing and shipping products. My ideal work environment consists of a collaborative multidisciplinary team, that shares my love for a good cocktail and coffee.
