Dinner Lab Email Campaigns


Since the native Dinner Lab phone app is still under development, communication with members is done through email. This is how all new event releases are made public and locations information is distributed. Since our members are accustom to receiving this type of communication, we wanted to develop automatic emails that would go out regularly to encourage member interaction at key points in their membership.

Role: Design & Development

Automating Emails

I worked closely with the marketing and data analytics teams to establish key points in a user’s membership based on our transactional data. This allowed us to set up a series of emails that we could automate to maximize member interaction through out their first year as a member. I was responsible for designing and coding the HTML emails and creating editable campaigns for our support staff to use in MailChimp.
