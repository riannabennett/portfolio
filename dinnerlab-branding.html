Dinner Lab Branding & Identity

Role: Project Manager, Lead Designer


As the lead designer at Dinner Lab, I have been responsible of crafting and developing the brand as we scaled from a handful of people working out of an apartment to a VC backed food + tech company with over 70,000 members. I worked with all aspect of the company to develop a cohesive branding strategy, managing the identity and user experience for all 31 markets across the county. It was crucial for us to unify our digital and event experiences, connecting members with the brand. For the corporate partnerships side of the business, I worked to establish clearly documented partnership brand guidelines to maintain Dinner Lab’s strong brand identity, even when working with large partners such as Bacardi, Pandora, and Saturday Night Live.
What is Dinner Lab

Dinner Lab is a full stack event experience company operating in 31 cities across the United States. The company discovers new culinary talents and ideas from the best kitchens, creating community around a unique kind of dining. By combining elements of a rotating dinner series (dinners are held in unique spaces that exist for only 24 hours) with diner engagement and feedback, Dinner Lab creates the dinner club of the future.

Having hosted over 2,500 events and built a foodie consumer base of nearly 75k, Dinner Lab is the largest culinary events company in the United States. Dinner Lab is more than your typical night out. It is an event experience company, with a logistical and technology backbone that has disrupted the industry, changing the way people think about eating out.

In addition to the consumer side of the business, Dinner Lab works with companies like Google, Mercedes Benz, and NASDAQ to create unique experiences for their guests in both conventional and unconventional spaces alike.

Brand Values

Our voice is vivacious, adventurous, and experienced. Always willing to try, even if it means failing. Friendly, never snobby. Knowledgeable but always open to learning. Community, above all.

Dinner Lab brings together like minded people for new culinary experiences. We are accessible. We firmly believe in supporting the growth and development in the culinary community. We believe that some of the best chefs are behind the scenes. We believe that good food, good drink and good people are all you need to make a memorable meal. We believe that people want to try new things. We believe that you don’t have to be a food critic to give valuable feedback to a chef - our members give chef some of the most valuable and direct feedback they can get.

The Industry

Although there are some competitors in the field, Dinner Lab immediately sets itself apart with the regularity of events, rotating venues, diversity of chefs, geographical reach, and accessible price point. Competitors in local markets do not have the capacity to handle the logistics of an operation like Dinner Lab and therefore do not have the opportunity to scale. Dinner Lab provides a one-night only experience. We are developing the chefs, connecting the best and brightest with our member community. With the largest member base of any supper club or catering network, our ability to deliver curated experiences across the country is second to none.
